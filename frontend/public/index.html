Welcome!

Many of us see natural water in our neighborhoods. Even small streams and ponds help clean our water and reduce flooding. Share a photo to support vital water resources.


Take a photo of water or a wetland. In under a minute, easily share it with this app.

Share a water photo

  1. Find one of your photos of water or a wetland.
  2. Click "Choose File" button, then select that photo.
  3. Click the green "Upload" button to submit.

Your photo will teach us about these vital water resources.


For easy photo tips, click right-arrow (below - next to "Details").

WHERE: Look for puddles or creeks during your day. Try a bridge with a sidewalk that crosses a natural or concrete waterway.

HOW - Tips:

  • Take the photo from a safe spot like a bridge's sidewalk, path, or other stable area.
  • Include plants or animals if you can.
  • Try to keep the camera steadier.

HOW MUCH: Even if a puddle is temporary, our minimum size is: filled kiddie pool, or if shallow: 1-inch deep oval that fills a parking space.
