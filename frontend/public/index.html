Try our Water Snap & Map!

Welcome!

Water is in small places many of us pass every day. Every stream, pond, and puddle helps clean our water and reduce flooding.

With this app, you take a quick photo of water or a wetland. Then easily share it in under a minute to help others learn.

Share a water photo

  1. Take a photo of water or a wetland.
  2. Click "Choose file", then select one photo.
  3. Click the green "Upload" button to submit your photo.
  4. Wait a moment while AI analyzes, then describes your photo.

Your photo will teach us about these vital water resources.


Click arrow at left of "Details" for easy photo tips.

WHERE: Look for puddles or creeks during your day. Try a bridge with a sidewalk that crosses a natural or concrete waterway.

HOW - Tips:

  • Take the photo from a safe spot like a bridge's sidewalk, path, or other stable area.
  • Include plants or animals if you can.
  • Try to keep the camera steadier.

HOW MUCH: Even if a puddle is temporary, our minimum size is: filled kiddie pool, or if shallow: 1-inch deep oval that fills a parking space.
